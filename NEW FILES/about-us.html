About Eagle Robotics

We are a team of passionate students, educators, and mentors dedicated to advancing robotics and inspiring innovation in our community.

About Us

We are BEST team 0058 Eagle Robotics from Allen, Texas. Our team operates out of the Allen ISD STEAM Center. We strive to strengthen our community through STEM and be the leading representative for inclusivity, diversity, and equity in our area. We serve our community in various ways, including mentoring rookie teams, hosting community events, and visiting schools. These experiences give our team members opportunities to be leaders, allow them to develop the necessary skills they need for success, and inspire others to pursue STEM careers.

Our Mission

To foster a love for STEM through hands-on learning and competition. We strive to build teamwork, creativity, and technical skills in our members.
