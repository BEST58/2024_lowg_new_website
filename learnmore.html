2024 Low G

The Low-G Best Robotics Competition is an exciting event where teams of students design, build, and program robots to compete in a series of challenges in a low-gravity environment. The competition encourages creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork, as participants apply engineering and programming skills to complete various tasks. It's a unique opportunity for aspiring engineers and roboticists to showcase their talents, learn about real-world applications of robotics, and engage in hands-on STEM activities.
